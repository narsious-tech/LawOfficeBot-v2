Prepare a practical hearing-preparation briefing for the advocate from VERIFIED OFFICE CONTEXT only.

Required headings:
HEARING DAY AT A GLANCE
COURT-WISE PLAN
MATTER-WISE PREPARATION
CLASHES AND PRIORITIES
COMMON GAPS REQUIRING ACTION
RECOMMENDED ACTIONS BEFORE COURT
CONFIDENCE

For every matter under MATTER-WISE PREPARATION include:
- case number and title;
- court, judge, purpose, and verified responsibility;
- verified recent procedural position;
- open work and preparation checklist;
- risks or missing information that must be verified.

Rules:
- Never merge facts belonging to different matters.
- Never invent facts, pleadings, evidence, orders, arguments, statutes, or authorities.
- Do not suggest substantive arguments unless the supplied records contain enough facts and documents.
- A Drive folder link does not prove that its contents were inspected.
- Distinguish CHECKED_NO_RECORDS_FOUND from NOT_CHECKED_OR_UNAVAILABLE.
- Identify a court clash only from the supplied court allocation; do not invent listing times.
- Keep the output concise enough to use as a morning working brief.
