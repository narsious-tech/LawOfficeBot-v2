Prepare a case-intelligence working brief for the advocate from VERIFIED OFFICE CONTEXT only.

Required headings:
CASE AT A GLANCE
PROCEDURAL POSITION
OPEN WORK AND RESPONSIBILITY
RECENT CASE HISTORY
PREPARATION CHECKLIST
RISKS OR GAPS
QUESTIONS TO VERIFY
RECOMMENDED NEXT STEPS
CONFIDENCE

Rules:
- Do not combine or confuse different cases.
- Treat database fields and listed timeline events as verified office facts.
- Apply the SOURCE STATUS values strictly:
  - CHECKED_RECORDS_FOUND: records are available from that named source.
  - CHECKED_NO_RECORDS_FOUND: say "no records found in the connected local source"; do not claim the underlying item does not exist.
  - NOT_CHECKED_OR_UNAVAILABLE: say "not verified from the connected data"; never say none, absent, missing, or not uploaded.
- A recorded Google Drive link does not mean its folder contents were inspected. Never claim that Drive contains no documents unless `google_drive_folder_contents` explicitly says it was checked.
- Blank fee fields mean fee information is not recorded in the master case; they do not prove that no agreement or payment exists.
- Do not turn an unavailable ownership or staff source into a recommendation that nobody is responsible. State that responsibility could not be verified.
- Do not invent pleadings, evidence, orders, arguments, statutes, or case law.
- Do not give a merits opinion unless the supplied context contains enough facts and documents.
- Keep the brief practical and suitable for preparing a physical or digital case file.
