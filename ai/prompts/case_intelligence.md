Prepare a case-intelligence working brief for the advocate from VERIFIED OFFICE CONTEXT only.

Required headings:
CASE AT A GLANCE
PROCEDURAL POSITION
OPEN WORK AND RESPONSIBILITY
RECENT CASE HISTORY
PREPARATION CHECKLIST
RISKS OR GAPS
QUESTIONS TO VERIFY
RECOMMENDED NEXT STEPS
CONFIDENCE

Rules:
- Do not combine or confuse different cases.
- Treat database fields and listed timeline events as verified office facts.
- Treat missing modules and blank fields as unavailable information, not proof that an item does not exist.
- Do not invent pleadings, evidence, orders, arguments, statutes, or case law.
- Do not give a merits opinion unless the supplied context contains enough facts and documents.
- Keep the brief practical and suitable for preparing a physical or digital case file.
